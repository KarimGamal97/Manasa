File storage

  • Home
  • File storage
The documents kept in the files are the beating heart of the organization, whether companies, government sector or non-profit Organizations may need an entity that hosts, stores, protects and manages files.

We work with you to provide a secure storage to save, protect and manage your files through a number of services:

  • Providing a secure storage equipped with security and protection means to save and store files
  • Provision of filing boxes
  • Inventory files and put barcode labels on boxes
  • Full and on-demand document scanning
  • Transfer, paging and delivery of files on request
  • Photocopying of paper documents
  • Provide a borrowing system and file storage management
  • Providing a cloud delivery and storage system
Documentation and archives center

Organizations need a main center for the preservation of documents and archives of the organization that contributes to their preservation and full security

We are working with you to establish a documentation and archives center within the organization:
  • Supply of various stationary, mobile and electric file cabinets
  • Supplying the documents and archives center management system for registering documents, controlling their loan and determining the duration of preservation and destruction
  • Supply of electronic archiving system and file sharing
  • Providing various devices from scanners, barcode printers and supplies
  • Provide the operation team of the documentation and archives center
  • Developing the policies and procedures of the documents and archives center based on the standards of the National Center for documents and Archives in the kingdom of Saudi Arabia
  • Secure destruction services for expired documents
